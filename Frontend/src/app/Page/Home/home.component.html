JEB Incubator

JEB is an innovation incubator dedicated to supporting ambitious, high-potential startups. We support entrepreneurs from their earliest stages and empower them to transform their ideas into concrete, viable, and impactful projects. Our mission is to uncover talent, connect project leaders with the right partners (investors, mentors, institutions), and foster a dynamic, supportive, and forward-looking ecosystem. Thanks to our expertise, network, and tools, we enable each incubated startup to gain visibility, accelerate its growth, and benefit from tailored support.
